# SEO Guide - Get Your App on Google

## Step-by-Step Guide to Appear on Google Search

### 1. Deploy Your App (Required First Step)

Deploy to Streamlit Cloud first to get your public URL:
- Your URL will be something like: `https://your-app-name.streamlit.app`

---

### 2. Submit to Google Search Console

**A. Go to Google Search Console**
- Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console
- Sign in with your Google account

**B. Add Your Property**
1. Click "Add Property"
2. Choose "URL prefix" option
3. Enter your Streamlit app URL: `https://your-app-name.streamlit.app`
4. Click "Continue"

**C. Verify Ownership**

Since you can't add HTML files to Streamlit Cloud, use these methods:

**Method 1: HTML Tag (Best for Streamlit)**
1. Google will give you a meta tag like:
   ```html
   <meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
   ```
2. I'll help you add this to your app after deployment

**Method 2: Domain Verification (If you have custom domain)**
- Use DNS verification
- Add TXT record to your domain DNS

**D. Submit Your Sitemap**
1. After verification, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
2. Add: `https://your-app-name.streamlit.app`
3. Click "Submit"
